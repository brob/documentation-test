The **Notifications** section informs you of comments assigned to you in content entries.

![Notifications](/images/docs/getting-started/notifications.png)

If you have unread comments, a red mark will appear on the bell icon. This section picks up notifications from all your Hygraph projects.

In the notifications section you can:

- Click on a comment to navigate to it.
- Mark notifications as read/unread
- Delete a notification

Click [here](/docs/guides/overview/commenting) to access detailed information on notifications and comments.
