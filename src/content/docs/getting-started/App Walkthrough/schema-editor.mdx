Your schema is defined by the models you create, and fields you add. It is the backbone of your Hygraph project. In the **Schema editor** you'll define the structure of your content by adding fields, creating relationships between models, and much more.

![Schema editor](/images/getting-started/getting-started-schema.png)

In the Schema editor you can:

- [Create a model](/getting-started/create-a-model)
- [Add fields to your model](/getting-started/add-fields)
- [Customize your sidebar](/guides/content/customize-sidebar)
- [Add components](/guides/schema/components)
- [Add remote sources](/guides/schema/remote-sources)
- [Add enumerations](/guides/schema/using-enumerations)
